About Site of Grace

At Site of Grace, we believe in nurturing not only the spiritual but also the practical growth of our youth. Inspired by the growing challenges young people face in finding meaningful work, we launched the Youth Employment Initiative. This initiative was born from a vision to empower our youth with skills, mentorship, and job opportunities that align with their talents and passions. Our goal is to help young individuals in the community become self-sufficient, confident, and equipped for long-term career success. The primary focus of this initiative is to provide hands-on training in various fields, connect participants with local businesses for internships, and offer workshops on essential skills like resume writing, communication, and financial literacy. We also aim to instill values of integrity, hard work, and service, shaping the youth not only as professionals but as responsible citizens. So far, we are proud to have placed dozens of young individuals into internships and part-time jobs, with several securing permanent positions. The program has seen a remarkable response, with both the youth and local employers praising its effectiveness in bridging the employment gap. However, we know there is more to be done.
